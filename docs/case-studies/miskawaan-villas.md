# Case Study: Miskawaan Beach Villas

## Client Profile

- **Client:** Miskawaan Beachfront Villas
- **Industry:** Ultra-Luxury Private Villas
- **Website:** miskawaanvillas.com
- **Relationship:** F2's newest client (signed March 2026)
- **Location:** Maenam Beach, Koh Samui, Thailand
- **Properties:** 11 private beachfront villas (4–7 bedrooms each)
- **Price Range:** $500–2,000+ per night
- **Recognition:** #1 Specialty Lodging on TripAdvisor Koh Samui, 5/5 Rating

## Scope of F2 engagement

**Microsoft 365 administration under a one-year SLA: March 2026 – March 2027.**

This is F2's first contract with Miskawaan and is scoped specifically to Microsoft 365 tenant administration. It is not a general IT-management engagement.

## Challenge

Miskawaan's growing team needed proper Microsoft 365 administration — user provisioning, mailboxes, security policies, and licence optimisation — handled by an experienced partner rather than as a side-task for already-stretched in-house staff.

As TripAdvisor's #1 specialty lodging on Koh Samui, the brand cannot afford guest-facing fallout from email outages, compromised executive accounts, or licensing missteps that interrupt staff productivity at a critical guest-experience moment.

## Solution

F2 took over Microsoft 365 administration under a one-year SLA running **March 2026 – March 2027**. Scope:

- **Tenant administration** — global admin role, configuration baseline, change management.
- **User lifecycle** — joiner/mover/leaver workflows, account provisioning and offboarding, group memberships.
- **Mailboxes & shared resources** — Exchange Online configuration, distribution lists, shared mailboxes, room and equipment calendars.
- **Security** — Conditional Access policies, MFA enforcement, suspicious-activity review.
- **Licence optimisation** — right-sizing across M365 Business Basic / Standard / Premium, true-ups at renewal.
- **Helpdesk** — first-line and second-line support for staff M365 questions.
- **Quarterly compliance reviews** — secure-score posture, licence audit, configuration drift report.
- **On-call response** within SLA targets.
