# Case Study: Putahracsa Hua Hin

## Client Profile
- **Client:** Putahracsa Hua Hin
- **Industry:** Boutique Luxury Resort
- **Website:** putahracsa.com
- **Relationship:** 10+ years
- **Location:** Hua Hin, Thailand
- **Properties:** 67 rooms & villas, 2 restaurants, bakery, spa
- **Recognition:** Five Star Alliance listed, design boutique

## Challenge
Design-focused boutique hotel where technology must be invisible to guests but reliable for operations. Multiple F&B outlets need integrated POS. Located 200km from Bangkok with limited local IT expertise.

## Solution
F2 manages full IT operations remotely from Bangkok/Samui:
- POS integration across 3 F&B outlets + spa
- Property-wide WiFi across all room zones and facilities
- Microsoft 365 for staff
- Domain & hosting for putahracsa.com
- Remote IT management with on-site visits as needed
- Hardware sourced through SiS partnership

## Results
- Seamless operations across all outlets
- Proves F2's remote management model works beyond Samui
- 10+ years, zero vendor changes
- Five Star Alliance standards maintained

## Strategic Significance
Putahracsa proves F2 can serve properties beyond Koh Samui — enabling expansion to Hua Hin, Pranburi, and the Gulf Coast corridor.

## F2 Services Used
1. IT Management Partner (remote + on-site)
2. Microsoft 365 Provider
3. Domain & Hosting
4. POS System Integration
5. Cybersecurity
6. Hardware Sales (via SiS)
