## Purpose

F2 Co., Ltd. ("F2") requests the Client's permission to include the Client's

prospective customers can see the range of properties F2 currently serves.

## What we would display

If you sign below, F2 may display the following on `https://f2.co.th`
(specifically the `/clients` page) and in related sales materials:

- Company name: **____________________________________________**
- Industry label (for grouping): **____________________________**
- Services provided by F2: _____________________________________

We will **not** display, without a separate written consent:
- Company logo or branded imagery
- Photographs of your property, staff, or guests
- Any quote or endorsement attributed to your team
- Any story describing the engagement, its challenges, or its outcomes
- Any commercial or contract terms

## Legal basis — Thailand PDPA (พ.ร.บ.คุ้มครองข้อมูลส่วนบุคคล พ.ศ. ๒๕๖๒)

Company names of juristic persons are not personal data under PDPA.
However, if the display incidentally identifies a natural person
(for example, the signatory below), that person consents under
Section 24 of the PDPA to the processing of their personal data solely
for the purpose of publishing this consent record.

## Withdrawal

The Client may withdraw this consent at any time by emailing
**dpo@f2.co.th** with the subject line "Withdraw client showcase consent".
F2 will remove the Client's name from all public surfaces within
**five (5) business days** of receiving the request.
